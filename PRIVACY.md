# Privacy Policy — Focus Flow

_Last updated: July 9, 2026_

Focus Flow ("the app") is a task, reminder, and focus app designed for ADHD brains. This policy explains what data the app handles and where it goes.

## The short version

**All of your data stays on your device.** Focus Flow has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, and no ads. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

## Data the app stores (locally only)

The following is saved in the app's private storage on your device and never leaves it:

- Tasks, to-do items, and nudges (reminders) you create
- Your streak history and usage events (used only to power the on-device "AI coach" suggestions)
- App preferences (theme, language, notification and sync settings)

Deleting the app deletes this data. You can inspect everything the app has recorded under **Settings → AI Insights → Inspect AI Memory**.

## Permissions the app requests and why

- **Notifications** — to show the task reminders and daily nudges you schedule. Optional.
- **Exact alarms** — so reminders fire at the exact time you chose, even when the app is closed.
- **Calendar (read/write)** — only if you enable "Sync to Calendar" in Settings. The app then adds/removes your own tasks and nudges in your device calendar. Calendar data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted anywhere. Optional; off by default.
- **Run at startup** — to re-register your scheduled reminders after the phone reboots.
- **Internet** — used solely to check a static version file for app updates (hosted on GitHub). No personal data is sent with this request.

## Data we collect

None. The app contains no analytics or tracking SDKs and does not use the advertising ID.

## Third parties

The app makes one network request: fetching a public version file from GitHub to offer you updates. GitHub may log standard connection metadata (such as IP address) as any web server does; see GitHub's privacy statement. No other third-party services receive any data.

## Children

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL with a new date.
